# Community Templates

Community templates are intentionally limited examples.

Production-ready pro templates are commercial assets and are not included in this repository. Commercial kits such as the GEO Launch Ops Kit, AI Coding Governance Kit, and enterprise governance rule packs may be referenced in documentation as product offerings, but their full template contents are not part of the public community repository unless explicitly stated.

Use this directory for small, non-proprietary examples that demonstrate BoundaryML concepts without exposing production-ready commercial assets or internal methodology.
